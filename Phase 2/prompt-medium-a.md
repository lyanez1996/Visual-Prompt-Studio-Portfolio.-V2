Prompt A – Wet Media Drawing

Create an artwork using watercolor and ink wash (wet media) that emphasizes line, color, contrast, and space. Use watercolor brushes, watered-down paint, and black ink pens, applying techniques like wet-on-wet blending, layering washes, and light cross-hatching to build texture and form. The composition should show a single tree standing alone in the middle of a desert with a low horizon line and open negative space around it.

Use soft sandy tones for the desert background, letting the wet media naturally blend and bleed to create subtle movement. Make the tree very vivid with bold, bright colors so it clearly stands out against the muted desert. Use strong contrast between the tree and the background to create emphasis, while keeping the overall scene minimal and balanced.
