Prompt B – Printmaking Version

Create an artwork using linocut printmaking that emphasizes line, shape, contrast, and space. Use a carved linoleum block, gouging tools, ink, and a brayer to apply ink before pressing the print onto paper. The composition should show the same single tree standing alone in the middle of a desert with a low horizon line and open space around it.

Instead of blended watercolor, simplify the image into bold carved lines and strong black-and-white contrast. Use thick and thin carved marks to define the trunk and branches, and leave large negative areas to represent the open desert. Focus on high contrast between the tree and the background to create emphasis. Keep the layout minimal, but let the visible carving marks add texture and character to the tree.
